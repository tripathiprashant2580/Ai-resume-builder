About Us

AI Resume Builder is a free, no-login web tool that helps job seekers and students create clean, professional resumes in seconds using AI. Whether you're building a resume from scratch or improving an existing one, our tool generates well-structured, action-oriented content across multiple designer templates — all directly from your browser.

The Service was built and is maintained independently, with a focus on being fast, free to start, and accessible to everyone — including students and early-career professionals preparing for their first job applications.

What We Offer

  • AI-assisted resume writing from your inputs (experience, education, skills, projects)
  • AI-based rewriting/improvement of an existing resume
  • Multiple professionally designed templates
  • Instant PDF download — no sign-up required
