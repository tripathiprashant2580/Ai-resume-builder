Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to any paid features or premium templates purchased on AI Resume Builder through Razorpay.

1. Nature of Our Products

All paid features on this Service (such as premium templates or unwatermarked PDF downloads) are digital products that are delivered instantly upon successful payment. Because the product is delivered immediately and in full at the time of purchase, we generally do not offer refunds once a premium feature has been successfully unlocked and used.

2. When a Refund May Be Considered

We will consider a refund in the following limited circumstances:

  • Payment deducted but feature not unlocked: if money was deducted from your account but the premium template/feature was not unlocked due to a technical error on our end.
  • Duplicate payment: if you were accidentally charged more than once for the same feature.

To request a refund under these circumstances, contact us within 7 days of the transaction with your payment reference ID (available in your Razorpay confirmation email/SMS).

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after a premium template or feature has been successfully unlocked and used.
  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated resume content, since content quality depends on the information you provide.
  • Failure to download your resume after successful unlock, if adequate time and opportunity to download was provided.

4. Refund Process & Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method via Razorpay within 5–7 business days of approval. Actual credit to your account may take additional time depending on your bank or payment provider.
