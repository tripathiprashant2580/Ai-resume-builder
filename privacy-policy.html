Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Resume Builder ("we", "our", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website to create or improve your resume.

1. Information We Collect

We are designed to require as little personal data as possible. Depending on how you use the Service, we may process the following:

  • Resume content you provide: name, contact details, work experience, education, skills, and any other information you type into the form or paste into the "Improve Existing" tool, solely to generate your resume.
  • Payment information: if you purchase a premium template or unlock a feature, payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not collect or store your card, UPI, or bank details on our servers — this information is handled directly and securely by Razorpay in accordance with their own privacy policy and PCI-DSS compliance standards.
  • Usage data: standard technical data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited, collected automatically via analytics and advertising services (see Section 4).
  • Cookies: small data files used for basic site functionality and by third-party services like Google AdSense (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate, display, and allow download of your resume.
  • To process payments for premium features via Razorpay.
  • To improve and maintain the functioning of the Service.
  • To display relevant advertising through Google AdSense.

Resume content you enter is sent to our AI provider solely to generate or improve your resume text, and is not used to train AI models or shared with any third party for marketing purposes. We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

3. Data Storage & Retention

The Service does not require account creation or login. Resume content you type is processed to generate your output and is not permanently stored on our servers beyond what is technically necessary to complete your request. We recommend saving your downloaded resume, as we do not maintain a history of previously generated resumes tied to your identity.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense: displays advertisements and may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can learn more or opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings and read Google's policy at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.
  • Razorpay: processes payments securely for premium features. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting & infrastructure (Netlify): our website and serverless functions are hosted on Netlify, which may log standard technical/server data.
  • AI processing: resume text is sent to a third-party AI API solely to generate or rewrite content on your request.

5. Cookies

We use cookies primarily to support advertising (Google AdSense) and basic site functionality. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; note that some features of the Service or ads may not function correctly without them.

6. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request details about any information associated with a payment you made, or request deletion of any data we may hold, by contacting us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
